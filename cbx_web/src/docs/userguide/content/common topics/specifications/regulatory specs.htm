Wi-Fi Module Certification

Depending on the customer's selection, the inverter may contain a Wi-Fi module and antenna operating in the 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz bands.

To prevent interference with other communication systems, the availability of some specific channels and/or operational frequency bands are site- and country-dependent. These settings are firmware-programmed at the factory to match the intended destination. The inverter must be configured either at the factory or during commissioning to match the intended destination, and the configuration must be based on local codes and standards. The firmware setting is not accessible by the end user.

Radio Frequency (RF) Exposure Information

The radiated output power of the wireless module is below the maximum allowable limits in the relevant standards from organizations such as ETSI, FCC, and Industry Canada. The wireless device should be used in a manner such that the potential for human contact during normal operation is minimized.
