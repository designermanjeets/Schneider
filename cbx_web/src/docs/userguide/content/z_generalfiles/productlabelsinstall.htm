Main Product Ratings Label

The main product ratings label contains the inverter's product ratings and technical specifications. Do not remove, cover, deface, or alter the main product label. A localized main product label is available to install on the product.

Front Panel Label

The front panel label contains information about the LED indicators and their behavior. It also identifies the various buttons and the port used in inverter operation.

Main Product Safety Label

The main product safety label is the main safety label for the inverter which lists general hazards and instructions on avoiding them. The label is applied on the exterior of the inverter on the section that is considered to be the inverter's front panel.

Do not remove, cover, deface, or alter the main product safety label. A localized label is available to install on the product.

For information on the symbols appearing in the label, see Label Symbols.

Other Safety Labels

Other safety labels appear on many areas of the equipment to warn of potential hazards within those areas of the inverter or to call attention to information that clarifies or simplifies a procedure. Read and follow all safety labels before proceeding.

Do not remove, cover, deface, or alter safety labels. Localized safety labels are available to install on the product..

For information on the symbols appearing in the label, see Label Symbols.

Section Identifier Labels

Section identifier labels can be found on each of the section doors making it easy to locate the subsystems of the inverter.

Other Product Component Labels

The inverter has other product labels that identify terminals, switches, breakers, fuses, ports, barriers, and other component parts.

Configuration Labels

These labels are specialized labels that are used during pre-commissioning and commissioning of the inverter. For example, the recombiner configuration label, used in pre-commissioning, is used to identify the inverter's grounding type and fuse ratings.
