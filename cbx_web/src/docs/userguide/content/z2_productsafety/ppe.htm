Personal Protective Equipment

All Audience must be equipped with appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) including the following:

  • Electrical-rated rubber insulating gloves with leather protectors (optional liners)
  • Safety glasses or goggles
  • Arc-rated long sleeve shirt and arc-rated pants or arc-rated coverall or arc-rated flash suit (minimum Arc Thermal Performance Value (ATPV) of 40 cal/cm2)
  • Hearing protection
  • Electrically-rated footwear
  • Arc-rated face shield with arc-rated hard hat and hood or arc flash suit hood (minimum ATPV of 40 cal/cm2)
  • Minimum 16 lock-out and tag-out (LOTO) locks and tags (one for external auxiliary AC disconnect, one for external main AC disconnect, 14 for the external PV disconnects)
  • Locking devices for miniature circuit breakers (example: Schneider Electric part number 26970)
  • Fall protection system for work at unprotected elevated heights

Check local safety regulations for other requirements.
